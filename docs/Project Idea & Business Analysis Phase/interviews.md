# Interviews

## Objective
To gather qualitative feedback from students regarding assignment management challenges.

## Interview Participants
- Student 1
- Student 2
- Student 3
- Student 4
- Student 5

## Interview Questions

1. How do you currently track assignments?
2. Have you ever missed an assignment deadline?
3. What challenges do you face in assignment management?
4. What features would help you manage assignments better?
5. Would reminder notifications be useful?

## Summary of Responses

### Common Challenges
- Forgetting deadlines
- Managing multiple assignments
- Lack of centralized tracking

### Desired Features
- Assignment reminders
- Calendar integration
- Progress tracking
- Course categorization

## Conclusion
Students expressed a strong need for a dedicated assignment tracking platform with reminder functionality.
